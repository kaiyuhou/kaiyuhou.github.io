About Me

I am a Senior Engineer of Kangaroo (RunD) Network Group at Alibaba Cloud now. My job focuses on improving networking foundations for cloud native systems, aiming to provide flexible and efficient networks within containers and above cloud VPCs.

I reveived my Ph.D. degress in computer science in 2022 from Northwestern University, under the guidance of Prof. Yan Chen.

Research Interests: I have a broad interest in various aspects of cloud networks, networked systems, and network protocols. My dissertation work focuses on network optimization of microservices, serverless based clouds. I am also working for introducing formal methods into network protocol verification.
