About Me

I am a 4th year Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science Department at Northwestern University, advised by Prof. Yan Chen.

Research Interests: I have a broad interest in various aspects of cloud networks, networked systems, and network protocols. My current research focuses on network optimization of microservices, serverless based cloud. I am also working for introducing formal methods into network protocol verification.
